What is infinite focus?

Earlier day’s look-at display needed the lens in your eye to adjust to the information you are trying to see. Infinite focus is described as, the light photons in the display been adjusted by the optical system. Your eyes directly sees through the display than looking at it. It is simple, easier and safer way to focus.
